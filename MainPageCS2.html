Our biggest Breakthrough with the Source 2 Engine

Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) is the highly anticipated sequel to the legendary Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO). Released by Valve Corporation, CS2 is a significant upgrade to the Counter-Strike franchise and runs on the Source 2 engine, bringing enhanced graphics, updated mechanics, and better overall performance.

  • Responsive Smokes

    Smokes are now dynamic and responsive, adapting to the environment and player interactions. Bullets and grenades can alter the smoke, creating new tactical possibilities in gameplay.

  • Source 2 Engine

    CS2 leverages Valve's Source 2 engine, offering improved visuals, including realistic lighting, shadows, and environmental effects. Enhanced player models, weapon textures, and map redesigns for a modern, polished look.

  • Sub-Tick System

    Unlike CS:GO, which relied on a tick-rate system for updates, CS2 introduces a sub-tick system, providing smoother and more precise hit registration and movement. Every action, like shooting or moving, is registered instantly.
